About

I'm based in Canada and joining Coefficient Giving as a recruiter. I wanted to get closer to the AI safety space—I care a lot about mitigating existential risks—and I'm also passionate about global health & development and animal welfare.

Beyond work, I'm involved in effective altruism—I've been a Giving What We Can member since 2017. I used to blog a lot on Medium (though some of my views may have changed since then). I also offer free web and app development for impactful organizations—I love building things. Currently working with Suzy Shepherd on a fake AI chat interface for her short film Seat at the Table.

I studied Commerce at Queen's University. I also enjoy photography—especially pictures of tree bark, inspired by Barkscapes.
