About

I'm based in Canada and starting at Coefficient Giving as a recruiter. I care deeply about mitigating existential risks, and working at the intersection of talent and high-impact work feels like the right place to be. I'm also passionate about global health and development and animal welfare.

Beyond work, I'm involved in effective altruism—I've been a Giving What We Can member since 2017. I write on Medium and offer free web and app development for impactful organizations—I love building things. Past projects include Aligned and a fake AI chat interface for Suzy Shepherd's short film Seat at the Table.

I studied Commerce at Queen's University. I also enjoy photography—especially pictures of tree bark, inspired by Barkscapes.
